# Email Bridge

The Email Bridge enables email-to-iMessage communication. Email lines use iMessage email addresses instead of phone numbers.

## How Email Lines Work

| Feature | Email Line | Phone Line |
|---------|-----------|------------|
| Pricing | $60/mo | $149/mo |
| Call button visible | No | Yes |
| SMS fallback | No | Yes |
| BYOD option | No | Yes |
| Daily limit (new) | 50 | 50 |
| Daily limit (total) | 150 | 150 |

## Setup

Email lines are provisioned automatically when you purchase one through the billing page. The iMessage email address is assigned from our infrastructure.

## Use Cases

- **Marketing blasts**: No call-back button means leads text instead of calling
- **High-volume outreach**: Lower cost per line
- **Multi-line setups**: Mix email + phone lines for different use cases

## API

Email lines work identically to phone lines in the API. The only difference is the `recipientEmail` field instead of `recipientPhone`.
