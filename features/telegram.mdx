## What Telegram Does

Connect Telegram to your Tuco workspace for:

- **Real-time reply notifications** with lead name and message preview
- **Reply directly from Telegram** — your response is sent as an iMessage to the lead
- **KB copilot drafts** with Approve/Reject buttons
- **Campaign notifications** and billing events

## Connecting Telegram

1. Go to **Integrations → Telegram**
2. Click the deep link to open the Tuco bot in Telegram
3. Send `/start` — your workspace is linked

### Per-Member Linking

Each team member can link their own Telegram account. Reply notifications are routed to the **contact owner's** personal Telegram, not just the workspace channel.

## Bot Commands

| Command | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| `/whoami` | Shows workspace name, plan, line count, member count |
| `/send +1234567890 message` | Send an iMessage directly from Telegram |
| Reply to notification | Your reply is sent as an iMessage to the lead |

## Knowledge Base Integration

When KB mode is set to **Copilot**:
1. Lead replies → KB generates a draft
2. Draft is sent to your Telegram with **Approve** / **Reject** buttons
3. Tap Approve → reply is sent to the lead
4. Tap Reject → discard the draft, reply manually

## Notification Routing

| Scenario | Where notification goes |
|----------|----------------------|
| Contact owner has Telegram linked | Owner's personal Telegram |
| No personal link | Workspace Telegram channel |
| No workspace Telegram | Email only |
| Global support | Always copied to support channel |
