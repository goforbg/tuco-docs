## Step 1: Set Up Your First Line

A "line" is your messaging channel. You need at least one active line to send messages.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Lines">
    Log in and go to **Settings → Lines**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Purchase or Configure Line">
    Click **Add Line** and follow the setup wizard
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Server URL">
    Ensure your line has a valid Server URL and shows as **Active**
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
**First-time setup:** Your first line configuration may take 2-3 minutes. You'll receive an email when it's ready.
</Note>

---

## Step 2: Upload Your First Leads

Now let's add some leads to your workspace.

### Option A: Use Sample Template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Template">
    Go to **Leads → Import** and click **Download Template**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill in Sample Data">
    Add 2-3 sample leads to the CSV:
    
    ```csv
    firstName,lastName,email,phone,companyName
    John,Doe,john@example.com,+12025551234,Acme Corp
    Jane,Smith,jane@example.com,+12025555678,Tech Inc
    ```
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Upload CSV">
    Drag & drop the file or click **Choose File**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create List">
    Name your first list (e.g., "Sample Leads")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Map Fields">
    Review auto-mapped fields and click **Import**
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Option B: Quick Send (No Upload Required)

Skip the upload and try Quick Send instead:

<Card title="Try Quick Send" icon="bolt" href="#step-4-send-your-first-message">
  Jump to Step 4 to send a message without uploading leads
</Card>

## Step 3: Check iMessage Availability

Verify which leads support iMessage before sending.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Leads">
    Go to **Leads** page - you'll see your imported leads
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Check Running">
    The system automatically checks availability after upload
    
    Watch the status icons:
    - 🔄 **Checking** - Verification in progress
    - 🔵 **Available** - Ready to receive iMessage
    - ⚪ **Unavailable** - Will receive SMS/email instead
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manual Re-check (Optional)">
    Select leads and click **Check Availability** to re-verify
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Pro Tip:** Availability checks take 5-10 seconds per lead. The system processes them automatically in the background.
</Tip>

---

## Step 4: Send Your First Message

Let's send your first message! Choose your preferred method:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Quick Send">
    **Best for:** Testing or one-off messages
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Quick Send">
        Click **Quick Send** button in the top nav
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Enter Recipient">
        Type a phone number with country code:
        
        `+12025551234`
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Wait for Availability Check">
        System automatically checks if recipient supports iMessage (5 seconds)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Compose Message">
        Type your message:
        
        "Hi! This is a test message from Tuco AI."
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Send">
        Click **Send Now** - Your first message is on its way! 🚀
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Send to Leads">
    **Best for:** Organized campaigns
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Leads">
        Go to **Leads** page and select one or more leads (checkboxes)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Open Message Composer">
        Click **Send Message** in the toolbar
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Compose Message">
        Type your message - same for all selected leads
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Choose Delivery">
        - **Send Now** - Immediate delivery
        - **Schedule** - Pick date and time
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Send Campaign">
        Click **Send** - Messages are sent sequentially
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Check>
**Success!** You've sent your first message with Tuco AI 🎉
</Check>

## Step 5: Track Your Messages

Monitor delivery status and engagement:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Messages Dashboard">
    Navigate to **Messages** page
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Message Status">

    - ❌ **Failed** - Delivery error

  <Step title="Filter & Search">

    - By status
    - By date range
    - By recipient
    - By message type
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does availability checking take?" icon="clock">
    **5-10 seconds per lead.** The system checks all addresses (phone + email + alternates) and stops at the first available one. Bulk checks are processed sequentially in the background.
  </Accordion>
    
  <Accordion title="What happens if iMessage isn't available?" icon="circle-question">
    **Messages automatically fallback to SMS or email.** The system uses the best available delivery method based on your recipient's capabilities.
  </Accordion>
    
  <Accordion title="Can I schedule messages for later?" icon="calendar">
    **Yes!** When composing a message, choose "Send Later" and pick your preferred date and time. Messages are processed by a cron job that runs every minute.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I know if my message was delivered?" icon="circle-check">
    **Check the Messages dashboard.** Each message shows real-time status: Sent, Failed, Scheduled, or Cancelled. Failed messages include error details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Quick Send and Send to Leads?" icon="arrows-split-up-and-left">
    **Quick Send** is for one-off messages without creating lead records (great for testing).
    
    **Send to Leads** is for organized campaigns with full tracking and list management.
</Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How many messages can I send at once?" icon="envelope-open-text">
    **Depends on your plan:**
    - Free: 100 messages/day
    - Pro: 1,000 messages/day
    - Enterprise: Unlimited
    
    All plans support bulk sending - just select multiple leads!
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
