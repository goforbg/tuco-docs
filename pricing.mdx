## AI Credits

AI credits power all AI features in Tuco:

- **AI Responder** (Knowledge Base auto-replies)
- **Sequence message generation** (personalized outreach)
- **Reply classification** (interested/not-interested detection)
- **Onboarding suggestions**

### How credits work

- **1 credit = 1,000 tokens** (input + output combined)
- A typical AI conversation uses ~2-3 credits
- Credits reset monthly on your billing cycle start date
- Usage is visible in the AI battery icon in your dashboard header

### Credit packs

Need more credits? Buy one-time packs from the Billing page:

| Pack | Price | ~Conversations |
|------|-------|---------------|
| 10K credits | $5 | ~65 conversations |
| 50K credits | $20 | ~330 conversations |
| 100K credits | $35 | ~660 conversations |

Credit packs are added instantly. Invoices are available in your Stripe billing portal.

### Usage alerts

You'll receive email alerts when your AI credits reach:
- **80%** — heads up to plan ahead
- **95%** — almost out
- **100%** — credits exhausted (AI features still work but may degrade)

### Fair usage

AI features continue working even after credits are exhausted. We send alerts so you can buy more credits or upgrade your plan, but we never hard-block your AI features.
