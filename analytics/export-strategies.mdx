## Overview

Tuco AI is designed to sit alongside your existing reporting, BI, and data tools.

You can safely move Tuco data into your own systems using the export options and documented interfaces available in your Tuco workspace.

No additional behavior changes are required inside Tuco once you have connected it to the destinations you control.

---

## What You Can Export

You can access and move the following types of information into your own systems:

- Message information (such as status and basic metadata)
- Lead information (such as contact details and attributes you store)
- Line information (such as basic configuration and usage indicators)

This data can then be combined with your existing CRM or product data in any way that suits your business.

---

## How It Feels to Use

Once connected, Tuco AI behaves like a stable data source in your stack:

- Data is available for reporting in your own tools
- Historical information remains available for analysis
- No action is required from you when Tuco makes internal improvements

If you change how your own reporting environment is set up, Tuco continues to present the same predictable information for you to consume.

---

## Reliability & Safety

Tuco AI is built so that exported data can be trusted for long‑term analysis.

Data is preserved safely, and changes inside Tuco are designed so that your established exports keep working as communicated in product updates.

If you need help designing a reporting setup around Tuco, our team can share high‑level examples of how other customers bring Tuco data into their own tools, without exposing any internal implementation.
