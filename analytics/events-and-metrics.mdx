## What You Can See

Tuco AI gives you clear, high‑level visibility into how your outreach is performing:

- Overall message activity and status.
- Campaign performance over time.
- Line usage patterns and basic health indicators.
- Reply counts and conversation volume.

The system automatically handles the underlying tracking so that you can focus on reading and interpreting results in your own tools.

---

## Campaign & Message Insights

For campaigns and messages, Tuco AI surfaces information that helps you understand:

- How many messages are being sent.
- How often messages reach recipients.
- How frequently people respond.

Data is preserved safely so you can look back over previous periods and compare campaigns without worrying about hidden changes in behavior.

---

## Line Usage & Capacity

Tuco AI makes it easy to see whether your lines are being lightly used or heavily used.

You can view simple indicators that show how active each line is and how that changes over time, so you can make confident decisions about when to add capacity or rebalance usage.

No action is required from you to keep these views up to date.

---

## Using Tuco Data in Your Dashboards

You can bring Tuco AI data into your own BI or analytics tools and treat it like any other stable source in your reporting stack.

Once connected, your dashboards continue to receive the same types of high‑level information, even as Tuco evolves internally.

If you are designing a new dashboard around Tuco, focus on showing:

- Overall volume trends.
- Campaign‑level outcomes.
- Line usage and reliability at a glance.
- Conversation and reply volume.

The system automatically handles the details behind these numbers.
